It boasts the ninth-largest student intake among self-financing engineering colleges in Tamil Nadu (AICTE approved). KSRCT empowers students and faculty through dedicated support cells, industry collaborations for practical exposure, and a high-performing placement cell. Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
The ECE program focuses on the study of electronic devices, circuits, communication equipment like transmitter, receiver, integrated circuits (IC), and basic electronics. Students learn about analog and digital transmission & reception of data, voice, and video, basic electronics, microprocessors, satellite communication, microwave engineering, antennae, and wave progression.
Sub-Branches:
- Communication Systems
- VLSI Design
- Embedded Systems
Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
The CSE program covers various aspects of computer science and engineering, including algorithms, software development, computer hardware, and the integration of these elements. Students learn programming languages, data structures, algorithms, computer networks, and software engineering.
Sub-Branches:
- Artificial Intelligence
- Machine Learning
- Cyber Security
Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
The EEE program deals with the study of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. Students learn about power generation, transmission, distribution, and the working of electrical machines and transformers, as well as electronics devices and circuits.
Sub-Branches:
- Power Systems
- Control Systems
- Renewable Energy
Mechanical Engineering (ME)
The ME program involves the design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. It combines principles of engineering physics and mathematics with materials science. Students learn about thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, material science, and machine design.
Sub-Branches:
- Thermal Engineering
- Manufacturing Engineering
- Automotive Engineering
Civil Engineering (CE)
The CE program covers the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment, including works like roads, bridges, canals, dams, and buildings. Students learn about structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, transportation engineering, and environmental engineering.
Sub-Branches:
- Structural Engineering
- Environmental Engineering
- Transportation Engineering
Information Technology (IT)
The IT program focuses on the study of the design, development, implementation, support, and management of computer-based information systems. Students learn about databases, networking, web technologies, and software development.
Sub-Branches:
- Data Science
- Network Security
- Web Development
Computer Science and Engineering (CSM)
The CSM program is a specialized stream of CSE that focuses on the integration of computer science and management. Students learn about software engineering, data management, business analytics, and information systems management.
Sub-Branches:
- Business Analytics
- Information Systems
- Software Project Management
Computer Science and Engineering (CSD)
The CSD program is another specialized stream of CSE that focuses on data science. Students learn about big data analytics, machine learning, data mining, and statistical analysis.
Sub-Branches:
- Big Data Analytics
- Data Mining
- Statistical Analysis
